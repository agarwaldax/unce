1789 Venture Lab

Our goal at 1789 is to provide space and support for young innovators seeking intentional growth. We are a launch pad into an action-oriented entrepreneurial community focused on creating and scaling great startups. Given a chance, we are eager to help entrepreneurs at all stages of their journeys.

Start at 1789, grow your venture and graduate to Launch on Rosemary Street, Chapel Hill's business accelerator.

Research Hub (Kenan Science Library)

Launch Chapel Hill is an international award-winning startup accelerator located in downtown Chapel Hill, North Carolina. Twice a year, we accept applications to our accelerator program from entrepreneurs who are committed to building their early stage businesses into a self-sustaining enterprises.

Our goal is to provide the tools and knowledge needed to decrease risks, reduce go-to-market time, and accelerate the growth of your start-up.
